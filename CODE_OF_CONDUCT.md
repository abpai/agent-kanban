# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in communication and reviews.
- Assume good intent and stay constructive when giving feedback.
- Focus on ideas, code, and outcomes instead of personal attacks.
- Help keep the project accessible to contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
- Personal insults or sustained hostility
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive behavior that makes collaboration unsafe or unreasonably difficult

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, commits, code, wiki edits, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers through the repository's available private contact channels. Please avoid using public issues for sensitive reports.
